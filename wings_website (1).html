About WINGS

WINGS is a social platform for Professional Muslim Women formed with the intention of paving the way for the holistic advancement of its members. Our members contribute their expertise to serve the weaker sections of society in general, and women and children in particular - irrespective of their religious or social stratification. Since our inception in 2016, we have been productively engaging in the holistic development of women and children across several districts in Kerala.

Vision

To build a fair and equitable society by empowering women and fostering their holistic wellbeing.

Mission

To empower and support women by providing educational opportunities, promoting career advancement, offering legal aid, and fostering wellness. We aim to create a supportive community where our members can thrive and reach their full potential in all aspects of their lives.

Our Objectives

A

Focus on physical, emotional, spiritual and professional upliftment of WINGS members and women at large.

B

Enlighten women about balancing their rights and responsibilities.

C

Contribute to building functional families which are conducive for a happy and healthy living.

D

Nurture a generation being committed to giving back to society.

Can You Be a Part of WINGS?

Yes, you can! If you are a Muslim woman qualified in any of the professional fields listed below and you have an innate passion to serve the society, you are welcome to the WINGS fraternity.

Eligible Professionals (Working or Non-Working)

  • Doctors: Government Recognized Medical Practitioners
  • Mental Health Professionals
  • Engineers
  • Advocates
  • Academicians (Head of Educational Institutions, Head of Departments, Doctorate holders, College Lecturers and above)
  • Government Officials
  • Professionally Qualified Social Workers (MSW)
  • Media Professionals (Visual & Print)
  • Chartered Accountants
  • Entrepreneurs with any of the above backgrounds
